About Me

I am a student and aspiring developer with an interest in software engineering, programming, and technology-based projects. I enjoy learning how systems work, solving technical problems, and building projects that combine logic with clean design. My experience includes coursework and hands-on practice with programming languages, website creation, and development tools.

Over time, I have worked on projects involving HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Java, C++, Unity, and database concepts. These projects have helped me understand how to structure code, design interfaces, and organize technical documentation. I value projects that are practical, visually clear, and easy for others to follow.

This website is part of presenting my work in a professional format. It allows me to share my background, display projects, and provide ways for people to connect with me. In the future, I hope to continue improving my technical skills and expanding the variety of projects included in my portfolio.
